Roll 2 dice

Protection Cards: 0

Rules:

You start with 1 point.

If the sum of the dice is even, you gain 2 points.

If the sum of the dice is odd, you lose 1 points.

If you roll two identical dice, you gain 4 points.

If the sum of the dice is 12, you gain 1 protection card.

If the sum of the dice is 2, you lose.

If the your score is below 0, you lose.

Do as many rolls as you can!
